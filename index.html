Santa's Shuffle

Instructions

  1. Prepare to think of everything you've ever wanted
  2. Type 5 things you want for Christmas on the cards
  3. Press the yellow star to submit
  4. Pick 5 Xmas boxes
  5. Press the star again and remember what you got
  6. Press the snowflake to try again with new items
  7. You win when you get any 5 presents or when you feel less is enough

    8. Important: Press the star only once each time you're supposed to and don't ask why :D
